MajorCycle — Transactional email templates

A single visual record of every email the app sends through Resend (Supabase auth emails are configured separately in Supabase and are not included here).

Source of truth: the code, not this file. These render from web/lib/email/* and web/app/(public)/contact/actions.ts. Every email shares one brand wrapper — renderBrandEmail() in brandEmail.ts (navy→blue gradient header, brand mark, disclaimer footer). The header mark below is drawn inline for portability; the real emails load the hosted email-icon.png. Sample names, dates and addresses are illustrative.

The 4th paragraph is status-aware. Shown above: paid subscriber. Other variants swap only that line:
· trial — “You’re on a free trial — the days you have left are saved, and you get them back if you sign back in before [date].”
· no subscription — the paragraph is omitted entirely.
